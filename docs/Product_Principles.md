# Product Principles

## 1. Never Lose the Thread

Every interaction should strengthen the account memory.

## 2. Continuity Over Documentation

The goal is not better notes. The goal is preserving context.

## 3. The User Speaks, the AI Organizes

The user should be able to recap their day without thinking about where information belongs.

## 4. Review Before Automation

AI suggests. Humans approve. Automation increases only after trust is earned.

## 5. One Source of Truth

Do not duplicate information. Connect it.

## 6. Relationships First

Tasks are byproducts. Relationships and account momentum drive outcomes.

## 7. Everything Becomes Intelligence

Voice, email, calendar, meetings, texts, photos, documents, and CRM activity are evidence.

## 8. Preserve the Story

Account history should read like a timeline, not a pile of disconnected notes.

## 9. Reduce Cognitive Load

Do not ask users to remember things the system can remember for them.

## 10. CRM-Compatible, Not CRM-Dependent

Salesforce should be a future system of record, not a required dependency for MVP.
