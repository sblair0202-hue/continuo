# Founder PRD: Continuo

## Category

AI-powered Field Intelligence Platform.

## One-Sentence Pitch

Continuo transforms field activity into organizational intelligence.

## Problem

Field teams create valuable relationship, account, and workflow context every day through meetings, site visits, emails, calls, and voice conversations. Most of that intelligence is lost because field professionals are mobile, driving, and too busy to manually document everything in CRM.

## Core Insight

Every field interaction creates intelligence. Most companies lose it.

Continuo captures, organizes, and activates it.

## Beachhead Market

Medical device and healthcare field teams.

Why:
- High-value relationships
- Complex account workflows
- Long implementation cycles
- Frequent driving and in-person meetings
- Poor CRM completeness
- Significant institutional knowledge loss
- Clear need for account continuity

## Expansion Markets

- Pharma
- Diagnostics
- Clinical specialists
- Medical science liaisons
- Home health business development
- Field service
- Construction
- Insurance adjusters
- Real estate
- Any mobile professional managing relationship-driven accounts

## Strategic Positioning

CRM stores records. Continuo preserves context.

## Differentiation

Continuo is not a CRM, note-taking app, dictation app, or generic AI assistant.

Continuo is:
- A field intelligence graph
- A continuity layer
- An account memory engine
- A voice-first operating system for mobile professionals
